Ruhbarb Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 cups ruhbarb, cut into 1" pieces
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 tbs. butter
  • 4 tbs. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. nutmeg
  • 2 eggs, well beaten

Instructions

Bake at 350-375 degrees for 35 to 45 minutes. Cook the ruhbarb raw.
