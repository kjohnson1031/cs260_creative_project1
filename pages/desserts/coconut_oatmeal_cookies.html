Coconut Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup applesauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 tbs. milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup quick oatmeal
  • 1 cup coconut

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease cookie sheet. Beat applesauce and sugars until creamy. Add egg, vanilla, and milk; mix well. Stir in oatmeal and coconut. Using rounded tablespoons, spoon drops of dough onto prepared cookie sheet, placing 2 inches apart. Bake 13 minutes or until edges are light brown. Let stand 5 minutes before removing to racks to cool.
