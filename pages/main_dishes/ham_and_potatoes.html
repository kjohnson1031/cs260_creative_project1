Ham and Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 baking potatoes
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 cups milk or light cream
  • 1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups cooked ham, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese

Instructions

Wash potatoes, but do not dry. Cook potatoes and cool slightly. Peel and slice. Melt butter in saucepan. Add flour and salt; stir until blended. Slowly add milk, stirring constantly. Cook serveral minutes, stirring until smooth and thickened. Stir in Worcestershire sauce, then potatoes and ham. Spoon in 1.5 quart baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Heat in 350 degrees oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Cook uncovered.
