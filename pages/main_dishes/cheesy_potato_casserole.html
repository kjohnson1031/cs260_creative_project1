Cheesy Potato Casserole

Ingredients

  • 7 or 8 medium cubed and peeled potatoes
  • 3/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 cans of cream of chicken soup
  • 1 small onion or 1 tbs. dehydrated onion
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup mild cheddar cheese

Instructions

Mix ingredients and place in a 9" by 13" pan. Cook at 350 degrees, 45 minutes covered. Sprinkle wiht crushed cornflakes. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, uncovered.
