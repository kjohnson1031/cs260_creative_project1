Lydia's Protein Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs. soy protein
  • 1 tbs. chia seeds
  • 1/3 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/8 cup raisins
  • 1/8 almounds
  • 1 1/2 cups cashew milk
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon

Instructions

Combine chia seeds, soy protein, milk, and cinnamon in a bowl with a whisk. Then, mix in the other ingredients.
