Kim's Omelet

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs. olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup sliced ham
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/3 cup cheese
  • 1/4 cup salsa verde

Instructions

Heat a frying pan to medium heat and add olive oil to the pan. Whisk the eggs together in a separate bowl. Before adding the eggs, saute the ham and mushrooms in the pan. Then, add the eggs and spread across the pan. When the eggs are cooked on the bottom, flip the omlet over. Add cheese to one half of the omlet. Then, remove from the frying pan, and add the spinach, avocado, and salsa verde.
