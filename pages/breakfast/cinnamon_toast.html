Cinnamon Toast

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter (softened, slightly melted)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 6-7 slices of bread-toasted

Instructions

Combine first three ingredients. Spread over toasted bread. Put under broiler about 3-4 minutes or until bubbly.
